How It Works

Verification is simple and confidential.

1

Submit Your Info

Fill out our short verification form or call us. We’ll ask for your insurance card details and basic demographics.

2

We Verify Benefits

Our team contacts your insurance provider to confirm what residential treatment services are covered.

3

We Walk You Through It

We call you back with a plain-English breakdown of coverage, any out-of-pocket costs, and next steps.

Self-Pay Options

No insurance? We can still help.

If you don’t have insurance or your plan doesn’t cover residential treatment, we offer self-pay arrangements and can help you think through financial options. Our admissions team will walk through costs transparently — no pressure, no surprise fees.

We also support Medicaid enrollment for clients who may qualify but aren’t currently enrolled.
