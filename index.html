How We Can Help

A full continuum of care.

From medically supervised detox through transitional community living — we walk with you through every stage of recovery.

Holistic, Person-Centered Treatment

Our clients are adults dealing with poly-substance abuse disorder, who may also experience chronic or urgent medical and mental health issues. We offer non-judgmental, non-biased, trauma-informed care.

Learn more

Detox with Residential

Medical withdrawal management and comfort medications for detoxification. A 28–30 day residential stay gives individuals time to heal, learn about addiction, and gain tools for long-term sobriety — with dual-diagnosis treatment for underlying mental health issues.

Learn more

Community Connections

After residential treatment, clients step down to the transitional level of care. This phase provides continued 24-hour support and helps clients re-integrate through participation in 12-step meetings.

Learn more

Transition Support

Clients attend clinical group sessions five days a week and receive peer support and case management services. The Residential Transitional phase lasts approximately four weeks.

Our Approach

Care built around the person, not the diagnosis.

We treat adults dealing with poly-substance use disorder who may also be managing chronic or urgent medical and mental health issues. Our care is non-judgmental, non-biased, and trauma-informed — delivered by clinicians who understand that every story is different.

Dual-diagnosis treatment addresses underlying mental health conditions alongside substance use, giving clients the best possible foundation for lasting recovery.
