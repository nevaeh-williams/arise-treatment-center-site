Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

ARISE Treatment Center is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We believe that access to information about addiction treatment and recovery should have no barriers, and we are continually working to improve the accessibility of our online content.

Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including:

  • Visual impairments, including low vision and blindness
  • Hearing impairments
  • Motor and mobility impairments
  • Cognitive and learning disabilities

Measures We Have Taken

Our website has been designed and built with accessibility in mind. Specific measures include:

  • Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard.
  • Skip navigation: A “Skip to main content” link is provided at the top of every page.
  • Semantic HTML: Pages use proper heading structure, landmark roles, and ARIA attributes to support assistive technologies.
  • Color contrast: Text and interactive elements meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements.
  • Alt text: All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text.
  • Reduced motion: Animations respect the prefers-reduced-motion browser setting.
  • Responsive design: The site is usable across a wide range of devices and screen sizes.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may not yet meet all guidelines. We are actively working to identify and resolve any gaps. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know so we can address it promptly.

Report an Issue

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website, or if you have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We take all accessibility feedback seriously and will work to resolve issues as quickly as possible.
