Who We Are

A home for men who are ready to heal.

ARISE Treatment Center is a CARF-accredited, 3.5 residential licensed adult male facility in Zionsville, Indiana — a safe, clean, drug-free environment built to help men gain the knowledge and strength to maintain long-term sobriety.

Our Mission

Empowering recovery through compassionate, evidence-based care.

Our mission is to empower individuals and families to overcome behavioral health challenges and achieve lasting recovery through compassionate, evidence-based, and person-centered care.

Our Vision

A leader in Central Indiana behavioral healthcare.

We aim to be a leader in behavioral healthcare in Central Indiana, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative practices, and unwavering commitment to the well-being of the communities we serve.

Our Core Values

Five values that guide every decision we make.

These principles shape how we care for the men who walk through our doors, how we treat one another, and how we engage with our community.

Compassion

We meet every person with empathy, warmth, and unconditional positive regard.

Integrity

We are honest, transparent, and accountable in all that we do.

Respect

We honor the inherent worth and dignity of every individual.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards of care, professionalism, and continuous improvement.

Collaboration

We work together — across teams, with persons served, with families, and with community partners.

Our Philosophy

Person-centered, always.

At ATC, each person served is the expert on his own life, goals, culture, recovery, and definition of success. Our role is to bring clinical expertise, evidence-based tools, and unwavering support in service of what you are working toward. We make this real through five commitments:

Dignity, Choice, and Voice

We honor each person’s preferences, goals, and informed choice throughout care planning and delivery.

Safety and Recovery

We maintain a safe, clean, drug-free environment and structure services to support sustained recovery and long-term sobriety.

Sobriety Support and Connection

We encourage collaboration and working the 12-steps to complement individualized clinical treatment and support long-term wellness.

Evidence-Based, Individualized Care

We use evidence-based curricula (CBT, RBT, Seeking Safety) and individual therapy at clinically determined intervals, tailored to each person’s needs, strengths, risks, and stage of change.

Diversity and Accommodation

We are committed to advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion — recognizing and valuing the unique experiences, cultural backgrounds, and perspectives that enrich our care environment.
